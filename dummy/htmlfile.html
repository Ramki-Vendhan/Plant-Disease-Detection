Powdery Mildew

Recommended Natural Remedies

  • 1
    Baking Soda Spray
    Mix 1 teaspoon of baking soda with water and spray on affected areas weekly to neutralize the fungal environment.
  • 2
    Neem Oil Application
    Apply diluted neem oil to affected areas to inhibit fungal growth and prevent spread while being gentle on plants.
  • 3
    Milk Spray
    Create a solution with a 1:9 ratio of milk to water and spray on the affected plants. The proteins in milk have antifungal properties.
  • 4
    Improve Air Circulation
    Ensure proper spacing between plants and prune when necessary to increase airflow and reduce humidity that fungi thrive in.
  • 5
    Remove Affected Leaves
    Carefully trim and dispose of infected leaves to prevent the fungus from spreading to healthy parts of the plant.
